Mailing Lists

The main communication channel is the mailing list. We prefer mails written in english, mails in german are very rare.

You have to subscribe to the list before posting to it.

IRC

IRC channel #fai on irc.debian.org, very good for quick help. This is a bilingual channel. We speak English and German.

Wiki

The FAI Wiki has lots of more infos, some tips and tricks and scripts from other people, but is often not up-to-date. You have to request an account via email or on IRC to edit or create new pages.
